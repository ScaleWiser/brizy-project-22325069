FAQs

Your questions, answered

How much does it cost to ask questions?

Whether you're a Founder or an Expert, ScaleWiser is 100% free to use.

Do experts get paid?

Experts are welcome to offer paid consultations through ScaleWiser and set their own rates. Bookings are scheduled and billed through the platform at which point ScaleWiser earns a small commission.

Can I browse expert profiles?

Of course! And you can ask open or direct questions. If you would prefer to speak directly with one expert based on their profile just send them a direct message or @ them in the question.

What kind of experts will I find?

We have verified experts across verticals: marketing, product, fundraising, etc - they're all here with more joining everyday.

What type of questions can I ask?

Anything from "How do I build a go-to-market plan?" to "Is this pitch deck investor-ready?" If it’s startup-related and you need clarity, you can ask it.

What happens when I get an answer?

If an answer hits the mark, you can message the expert directly to book a 1:1 consultation at a time that suits you. Instant momentum.

I'm an expert, what's in it for me?

Visibility. Credibility. And paid consultations. Share what you know, build your reputation, and turn advice into income.

Do I need to know exactly what I'm asking?

Nope. Ask it how you’d ask a mentor or colleague - we’ll make sure it gets in front of someone who gets it. The more information you can provide however, the more targeted answers will be from experts.

How are we different from forums or Reddit?

Forums give you opinions from anonymous users who could be spouting nonsense in their free time. We give you answers. Every response on ScaleWiser comes from a vetted expert who’s walked the walk.
